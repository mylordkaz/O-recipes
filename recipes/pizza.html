Pizza margherita

The Classic Margherita pizza

In this recipe we gonna make a pizza margherita, it is very simple, pizza margherita are the base of almost any pizza.

It is up to you to add your favorite ingredients on the top, but then it will not be a margherita anymore. it will become your own original pizza.

Ingredients :

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Basilic

Here are the steps:

  1. Start by lay down the dough on a plate, make it like a circle
  2. Put the tomato sauce on all the surface
  3. Put some pieces of mozzarella randomly everywhere
  4. Now is time to put in the oven:
    • abour ~25min at 200 degrees

And we are done, simple and fast Your home-made pizza margherita

Enjoy!
