Pasta Carbonara

very tasty Pasta Carbonara

In this recipe we gonna make a home-made pasta Carbonara.

You can use any kind of pasta, but we recommend spaghetti, for the cheese parmesan cheese is the best.

Ingredients :

  • pasta
  • eggs
  • cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Sauce
  • ham cube

Here are the steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water for 5min (al dente).
  2. In a pan cook the ham with the cream.
  3. Add the pasta too the pan and mix together.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and the egg york.

And we are done, simple and fast Your home-made Pasta Carbonara.

Enjoy!
