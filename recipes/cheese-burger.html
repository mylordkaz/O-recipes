Cheese Burger

Ultimate Cheese-Burger with extra bacon!

In this recipe we gonna make a home-made Cheese-Burger, a fresh bun, with beef patty, cheese, salad, onion, and as you can see above some extra bacon slice.

It is up to you to add your favorite ingredients, here we just gonna give you our favorite version of the Cheese-Burger.

Ingredients :

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Bacon

Here are the steps:

  1. - Start by cooking the patty and the bacon in a pan, then reserve.
  2. - Cook also the onion in a pot with butter and the sauce.
  3. - Cut the bun in half and heat up the inside face using the pan.

  4. - Now is time for assembled from the bottom:
    • bun, cheese, patty, cheese, salad, onion, finish with bacon slice then close with the other half of the bun.

And we are done, simple and fast Your home-made Cheese-Burger.

Enjoy!
