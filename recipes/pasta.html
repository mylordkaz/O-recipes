Pasta

very tasty Pasta

In this recipe we gonna make a home-made pasta, i like bolo but you can add what ever you want in it.

You can use any kind of pasta, me i will go with spaghetti, for the cheese parmesan cheese is my favorite.

Ingredients :

  • pasta
  • eggs
  • tomato sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • ground beef
  • fresh cream

Here are my steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water for 5min (al dente).
  2. In a pan i cook the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  3. I add tomato sauce and the cream then let it heat up
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and the egg york.
  5. And at last the pasta and mix together.

And we are done, simple and fast Your home-made Pasta.

Enjoy!
